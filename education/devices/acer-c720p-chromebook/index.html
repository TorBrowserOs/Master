Features

For hands on learning

Whether you're tapping, swiping, or zooming, get things done with the latest touchscreen Chromebook. The C720P has up to 7.5 hours* of battery life and slips easily into a backpack. At school or on the go, it keeps up with your educational needs.

* Battery life may vary upon usage and other conditions.

For more doing and less waiting

The Acer C720P is easier to use than traditional computers you may be used to -- there’s no clutter, no waiting, and no nagging. It starts up in 7 seconds, and automatically stays up-to-date without interrupting you. Virus protection is built-in, guarding you against viruses, malware, and other nasties.

For the best of Google

Your favorite Google Apps are built-in and one click away. Find thousands more educational apps in the Chrome Web Store. And, you’ll get 100GB of Google Drive free for 2 years* to safely store all your stuff.

* 100GB of free storage for two years, starting on the date you redeem the Drive offer.
