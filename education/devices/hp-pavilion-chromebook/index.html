Features

Enjoy the big screen

The HP Pavilion Chromebook offers a brilliant 14’’ screen, making it easy for students and teachers to research, collaborate on documents, and watch videos in detail. Connect all the peripherals you want with a HDMI port, 3 USB ports, and a multi-format digital card reader.

Best of Google

Your favorite Google products are built-in like Search, Docs, Gmail, and Calendar so you can learn, work, and do whatever you want, right out of the box. It updates itself automatically and comes with virus protection. No extra software (or worrying) required.
