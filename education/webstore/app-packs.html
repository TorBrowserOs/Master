Chrome Apps for your classroom

Enjoy a bulk discount when you purchase 20 or more licenses
for apps in your Chromebook classroom.

In order to purchase / upgrade apps, you need to access this page via your Chromebook management console.

Instructions

  1. Visit the management console and Buy or Upgrade 20 or more app licenses. A bulk discount will be applied.
  2. After purchase and activation, install the apps for your group of users.
