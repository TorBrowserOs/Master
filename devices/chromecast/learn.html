Try your first cast

Use your phone or tablet to cast your ﬁrst video from YouTube to your TV in three easy steps:

1. Download the latest YouTube app on your Android phone or tablet, iPhone or iPad

2. Connect your phone or tablet to the same Wi-Fi network as your Chromecast

3. Open YouTube on your phone or tablet, touch the cast button, and play a video

Cast from a laptop

How to cast a website to your TV, including photos, video, and music

1. Install Chrome browser on your laptop

2. Open Chrome, and install the Google Cast extension

3. Connect your laptop to the same Wi-Fi network as your Chromecast

4. Go to any website, such as youtube.com, click the cast button, and watch

Things to try casting:

  • Photos from social media or photo-sharing sites
  • Videos from YouTube*, Netflix*, or HBO GO*
  • Online music, maps, news, sports and more

System requirements:

  • Windows 7 or later, Mac OS X 10.7 or later, or Chrome OS
  • Mirroring web video requires a recent laptop and strong Wi-Fi
  • Silverlight, Quicktime, and some other video plugins not supported

* These websites are optimized for Chromecast. Casting other websites is currently a beta feature.
