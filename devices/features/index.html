Switch between accounts in a snap or let someone new sign in. All your stuff stays separate.

       

Guest mode

Supervised users

Plays nicely with others

Works with your other devices – your phone, tablet or an old computer – without any extra cords.

   

Take a photo on your phone then edit it on your Chromebook. With Google+ Photos, your snapshots sync automatically.

 

Print from anywhere using Google Cloud Print. Your Chromebook connects to your printer through the cloud.

 

Access the software on your PC or Mac from your Chromebook by using Chrome Remote Desktop.

Gives you peace of mind

Virus protection built-in. No annoying updates. Chromebooks run Chrome OS, an operating system that’s fast, simple and secure.

Built-in protection

Virus protection, multiple layers of security, and verified boot help keep you safe from viruses, malware and other computer nasties.

Keeps your files safe

With Google Drive built-in, your files and photos are automatically backed up. You get 100GB of storage, for free*, with all new Chromebooks.

Stays fresh, all by itself

Chromebook updates itself, for free. So you always have the latest and greatest version. No need for manual downloads and upgrades.

* 100GB of free storage is for 2 years, starting on the date you redeem the Drive offer. Learn more.
