Chromebooks for your business

Faster. Easier. More Secure. Chromebooks are instantly personalizable and easy to manage, making them an ideal solution for business.

  • Bring employees online
  • Computers for customers
  • Equip mobile employees
  • Virtualize your desktop

Bring employees online

Many employees lack computer access due to the costs and logistical challenges of supporting remote locations. Chromebooks make it easy to…

  • Scale access to business apps with computers that are simple to use
  • Easily customize Chromebooks for use by multiple employees
  • Give employees access to their applications, files and settings whenever and wherever they sign in
  • Add computers and locations without adding significant IT work

Computers for customers

Customers already spend most of their time on the web, which makes it frustrating when they don't have a device they can use to connect. Chromebooks provide...

  • A personalized experience to all customers just by signing in
  • Freedom from the dangers of viruses and malware
  • All customer data can be wiped automatically on sign out

Equip mobile employees

Employees are on the go more than ever, whether they’re working in the conference room down the hall or from a cafe around the world. But mobile employees can be easily sidelined by viruses and lost files. Chromebooks offer mobile employees...

  • Fast start-up time
  • Share among employees with virtually no machine set-up for different users
  • Constant connectivity through integrated WiFi and optional 3G (plus support for secure WiFi and VPN)
  • Access to all of their work, data, and applications in the cloud, just by signing in

Virtualize your desktop

Virtual Desktop Infrastructure (VDI) is revolutionizing the way companies equip locations and individuals that lack access to internal business applications. Chromebooks can help you implement VDI through...

  • Trusted VDI partners and software such as those provided by VMWare®, Citrix®, Ericom and nGenX
  • Vendors, contractors and overseas operations can access virtualized versions of internal business applications
  • Chromebooks are cost-effective VDI end-points, but can also run web apps
