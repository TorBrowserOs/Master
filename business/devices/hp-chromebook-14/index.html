Features

For the big picture

Comfortably edit an image-rich presentation or video chat with your entire team using the brilliant 14” display. And with up to 9.5 hours* of battery life, you can be on the go all day without needing to charge.

* Battery life may vary upon usage and other conditions.

For more doing and less waiting

The HP Chromebook 14 is easier to use than traditional computers you may be used to -- there’s no clutter, no waiting, and no nagging. It starts up in 7 seconds, and automatically stays up-to-date without interrupting you. Virus protection is built in, guarding you against viruses, malware, and other nasties.

For the best of Google

The top business apps are built-in and one click away. Find thousands more in the Chrome Web Store. And, you’ll get 100GB of Google Drive free for 2 years* to safely store all your work.

* 100GB of free storage for two years, starting on the date you redeem the Drive offer.
