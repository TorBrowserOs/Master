Features

Enjoy the big screen

The HP Pavilion Chromebook offers a brilliant 14’’ screen, making it easy to work with videos and photos in detail. Connect all the peripherals you want with a HDMI port, 3 USB ports, and a multi-format digital card reader.

Best of Google

Your favorite Google products are built-in like Search, Gmail, and Hangouts so you can be productive right out of the box. It updates itself automatically and comes with virus protection. No extra software (or worrying) required.
